What ideas can be submitted?

You can submit absolutely any idea: we do not want to limit employees. But we must understand that not every idea will be realized in the end. Whether we can implement the idea depends on many factors. Here are some of them: • Compliance of the idea with the requirements of the legislation of the Russian Federation; • Compliance of the idea with the values of Vkusville; • Novelty of the idea: are they unique or are they already being implemented/have there been experiments before; • For business/optimization ideas — the presence of economic potential; • For ideas that are strongly tied to the processes of existing directions, an expert assessment of the leaders and teams of these directions will be important (for example, Retail, Loyalty, Technology, Ways, URZ, etc.) Submit any ideas and get feedback — let this be the first step to finding the very idea that will take off!
